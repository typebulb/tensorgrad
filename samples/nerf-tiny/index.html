NeRF-tiny

A 4-layer MLP that learns the mapping (x, y) → (r, g, b) for one image. Watch reconstruction quality climb as the network fits the target. The headline trick is sinusoidal positional encoding — sin / cos at L frequency bands — without which a plain MLP can only fit the low-frequency part of the signal.

initializing…

Architecture: positional encoding (L = 8 bands, 32 features) → 3 × Linear(64) + ReLU → Linear(3) + sigmoid. Adam, MSE loss in pixel space, batches of 1024 random pixels. The model has ~10K parameters and converges to a recognisable image in a few seconds.
