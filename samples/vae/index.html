VAE on MNIST

Variational autoencoder. The encoder maps each digit to a distribution over an 8-d latent space; the decoder reverses it. The reparameterization trick (z = μ + σ·ε, ε ~ N(0, 1)) keeps the gradient differentiable through the sample.

Latent-space interpolation

Architecture: 784 → 256 → 256 → (μ, log σ²) [latent 8], decoder mirrors. Loss = sum-pixel MSE + KL against N(0, I), β = 1. Random-sample quality improves rapidly in the first few thousand steps; the interpolation slider becomes smooth once the latent manifold is well-learned.
