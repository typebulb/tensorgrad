CartPole REINFORCE

A two-layer policy MLP learns to balance an inverted pendulum on a cart via discounted-return-weighted log-policy gradients. No supervised target — the agent learns from its own actions and the resulting rewards.

initializing…

16 parallel environments stepped in lockstep for up to 200 steps per rollout, then one Adam step on the K-trajectory batch. Returns are discounted (γ = 0.99) and normalized for variance reduction. A random policy gets ~20 steps before the pole falls; converged policies run the full 200.
