Tic-Tac-Toe self-play

A small policy MLP plays itself, with the terminal outcome (+1/0/-1) used as the gradient signal for every move that player made. Play against the model while it trains and watch it get harder.

initializing…
 

Architecture: state encoded as 3 binary channels × 9 cells (empty, mine, theirs) from the current player's perspective, then 27 → 64 → 64 → 9 with tanh activations. Outcomes are normalized over the rollout for variance reduction. 16 parallel self-play games per rollout. A converged model never loses; the win+draw rate vs a random opponent reaches 100% quickly. Reaching optimal (all games drawn between two perfect players) is the limit.
