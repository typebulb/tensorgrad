Flow-matching-tiny

A tiny rectified-flow / flow-matching model trained from scratch on MNIST. Same conv stack as the diffusion sample — what differs is the training objective (regress on the data→noise velocity field) and the sampler (Euler integration backward in time, fewer steps).

Architecture: 4 stride-1 3×3 convs at 28×28, learnable timestep embedding added to feature maps. Training forms x_t = (1−t)·x_0 + t·x_1 and regresses on the velocity target v* = x_1 − x_0. Sampling Euler-integrates dx/dt = v_θ(x, t) from t=1 down to t=0 over SAMPLE_STEPS=25 steps. MNIST data is fetched from solenya-media S3 on first load.
